Score Music Studios

Private and Class Music Lessons for Kids and Adults Guitar, Voice, Piano, Ukulele, Trumpet, Saxophone, Drums, Percussion, Flute, Clarinet, Cello and More!

Our Mission...

...is to foster a love and appreciation of music and the arts. Score Music is an innovative music school in Sandwich, Massachusetts on Cape Cod. Our highly qualified teachers encourage their students to learn music they love so they enjoy practicing and remain motivated. Learn to sing, play piano, keyboard, guitar, bass guitar, ukulele, saxophone, clarinet, flute, trumpet, French horn, violin, cello, drums, percussion, and more! We also offer an Introduction to Music Class for kids ages 4,5 and 6 , Guitar and Ukulele Classes and Rock Band. We offer frequent performance opportunities many in the form of student recitals. Private Lessons are $28.50 per half hour. For more information about lesson and class rates, click here.

Score Music has been serving Cape Cod's families since 1996. Long time Score Instructor and Cape Cod musician Steve Gregory became the new owner on January 1st, 2013 and is committed to excellence in music education.

Learn to Sing, Learn to Play, Build Brain Power, Be Creative, Express Yourself, Join a Band, Have Fun!
